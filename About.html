Why did we create this website?

This website was created as a result of our inspiration, which can include a mix of personal interests, professional objectives, a desire to make a contribution to a particular subject, or a requirement to fill a void in the online environment. Depending on the person and their particular situation, the reasons can differ drastically.

Who is this website for?

It is essential to make sure that our website is accessible to everyone. We are fostering an inclusive digital space that serves people with a range of needs and disabilities by making our website accessible. This accessibility enables users of all abilities to access, explore, and interact with the content on your website.

Who are we?

As teenage entrepreneurs we have a unique opportunity to make a positive impact on the world. Teaching meditation poses and breathing techniques can be a great way to promote well-being and help people improve their mental and physical health.

Engineering design process

For the design process we first empathized and defined what the actual problem was and we then brainstormed some ideas in hopes of solving the issue at hand, and finally we designed a prototype and coded it into our website.
